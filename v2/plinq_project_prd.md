# PRD: Plinq - Gamified Trust Verification Platform

## 1. Project Overview
Plinq is a safety-focused platform designed to empower women (and men) by providing verified information about potential partners or connections. The core value proposition is "Safety through Information," delivered through a gamified verification funnel that transforms data lookup into a rewarding, trust-building experience.

## 2. Target Audience
- Primarily women seeking to verify the background and safety of personal connections.
- Individuals prioritizing personal safety and proactive risk management in social/dating contexts.
- Community members who value collective safety reporting and validation.

## 3. Core User Experience (Gamified Funnel)
The platform is structured as a linear, high-conversion funnel:
1.  **Entry (Landing Page):** Minimalist landing focused on the value of information and a clear CTA to start verification.
2.  **The Quiz (Data Input):** A multi-step, gamified process to collect partner data (Name, Phone, City).
    - Progress bars and step-based navigation.
    - Trust signals (LGPD compliance, confidentiality guarantees).
    - XP rewards for completing steps to incentivize thoroughness.
3.  **Processing (The Engine):** A high-perceived-value waiting screen demonstrating active data cross-referencing and background checks.
4.  **The Result (Trust Report):** A professional "Trust Score" (0-100) with detailed breakdowns of verified identity, criminal background, and social footprint.

## 4. Key Features & Screens
- **Dashboard (Home):** Summary of user status, safety level (XP), and daily proactive safety missions.
- **Verification Funnel:** Steps for selecting search type (Gender), inputting details, and processing.
- **Trust Report:** Comprehensive background check results with sharing capabilities.
- **Community Alerts:** A localized feed of community-validated safety reports and "Safe Points" (cafés, refuges).
- **Safety Profile:** Central hub for panic buttons, emergency contacts, and personal safety statistics.

## 5. Design System: "Empower & Protect"
- **Visual Style:** Modern, clean, and feminine using soft Glassmorphism.
- **Color Palette:** 
    - **Primary:** Deep Magenta (#ec4899) for actions and energy.
    - **Secondary:** Lavender and Soft Blue for background surfaces and trust.
    - **Alerts:** Red for emergency (Panic Button), Yellow for warnings.
- **Typography:** Plus Jakarta Sans for a modern, accessible feel.
- **Tone & Voice:** Empowering, professional, confidential, and proactive.

## 6. Gamification Mechanics
- **Experience Points (XP):** Earned through verifications, safety missions, and community contributions.
- **Levels/Ranks:** Progressing from "Protectora" to "Elite Verified."
- **Badges/Medals:** Visual recognition for specific achievements (e.g., "Night Watchman," "Community Leader").
- **Trust Score:** A numerical representation of a profile's verified safety.

## 7. Technical & Compliance Standards
- **Data Privacy:** Strict adherence to LGPD/GDPR standards.
- **Confidentiality:** All searches must be 100% anonymous to the subject.
- **Responsiveness:** Primarily mobile-first design for real-time safety utility.
